THE STATE SCHOOL

Empowering Student Knowledge

In State school, we create an environment where your child can discover a whole new world of knowledge, where the possibilities of imagination are endless, and where each student explores ever-broadening horizons. Here, we encourage students to creatively engage to become problem solvers, critical thinkers, and innovators. In an effort to make the students be empowered and successful beyond the classroom, we encourage them to be creative and develop different skillsets. Some of focus areas are:

  1. Problem solving
  2. Critical thinking
  3. Innovation

Key highlights of our curriculum

  1. Primary Curriculum
    • Increasing grasp of essential subjects is the main focus.
    • Boosting self-confidence through activities like public speaking.
  2. Secondary Curriculum
    • Incorporating thorough subject kits created by MIT grads and PhD holders.
    • The beginning of language instruction, logical thinking, and creative writing.

Who Are We

Learn About Our Work and Cultural Activities

Since our first school branch expanded in 2002 into a chain of more than 60 international schools, we have impacted the lives of more than 50,000 students and established a new standard for educational excellence. 'Shaping minds, touching lives, building second homes' is the school's motto. Along with academic performance, we place a high focus on personality development, creative approach, future-oriented and collaborative learning.
